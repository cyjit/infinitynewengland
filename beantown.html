Mark your calendars for our 5th year!

Since 2022, the Beantown Beatdown has stood as the Northeast's premier Infinity Satellite tournament. Join us at the Everwinter Gaming Convention.

We are committed to giving you the best 2 days of Infinity possible, prioritizing excellent sportsmanship, fabulous prizes, and some of the best game tables at any event. Whether you are a casual kitchen-table player attending your first event or a top-ranked player in the world, there is a seat for you here. See event coverage on the Corvus Belli blog.
