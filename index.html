Event Support

The best scenes are built by many hands. If you're organizing — or thinking about organizing — an Infinity tournament or convention in the Northeast, we want to be in your corner.

We offer help with event logistics, promotion through our platforms, and sourcing prize support. Whatever stage you're at, let's connect and make it happen.
